~ Main / Index / Html

The html output is intended to replicate the look of Markdown.
  • It needs the hard-coded file style.css
  • It uses the standard tags, h1 , h2 , h3 , h4 , h5 , h6 , a , b , i , p , div , pre , img , code , span , blockquote
It splits the document into two primary regions. Defined by the tags:
  • p: paragraph
  • span: end-paragraph
  • div: document
  • div: document-header
  • div: navigation-bar
  • div: document-content
  • code: typewriter
  • span: key-item
  • div: list-heading
  • span: list-separator
  • span: value-item
  • span: inline-text
  • span: end-paragraph
  • div: code-block
  • div: section-bar
