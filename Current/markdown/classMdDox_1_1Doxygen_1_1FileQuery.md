<span class="bold-text"><b>docFileType</b></span>
<span class="inline-text"> scaffolding.</span>

```xml
<xsd:complexType name="docFileType" mixed="true">
  <xsd:group ref="docTitleCmdGroup" minOccurs="0" maxOccurs="unbounded"/>

</xsd:complexType>
```
